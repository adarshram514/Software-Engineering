A little About Us

Daria Bilash

As a computer science major, my interest in developing Hindsight was sparked by my own journey as a young investor navigating the stock market. I recall feeling overwhelmed and unsure, with limited resources at my disposal and a reluctance to seek help. With Hindsight, my aim is to create a platform tailored to support young individuals as they enter the world of investing. By simplifying complex financial terms and concepts, Hindsight empowers users to approach the stock market with confidence. My hope is that Hindsight will reduce confusion and enable users to make informed decisions, thereby helping them capitalize on the opportunities presented by the stock market.
